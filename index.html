Brainstorming ideas
Self driving bus/train
  • Uses the same type of technology used for tesla cars
  • Since its at a much larger scale and more people are on board some tweaks would have to be made to the auto drive technology to ensure safety
  • Can always be switched to manual driving
Alerted stop
  • App that alerts a passenger when to get off their stop
  • Helps any older or sleeping passengers on public transportation
  • Uses the maps app to calculate the stop inputted into the app so that it alerts the passenger better
Sensors on train doors
  • Instead of the train conductor having to control the doors after opening the doors sensors will be able to notice if someone is trying to get in or out
  • Sometimes passengers make it to the train but the doors close, the person would be able to stick their hand in or out(depending on if they are leaving or entering) to stop the doors before it touches them
  • Normally the door has to slightly close on your arm until the train conductor of the cart notices If an item blocks the door someone could just move it aside
Pressure plates
  • To stop suicides and fatalities there will be pressure plates will be in between the train tracks and will be alerted if someone is on it notifying the coming train
  • A person can then safely go down and help the person below
  • The pressure plate will only detect a certain amount of weight so no rodents can cause any delays for trains
Barrier
  • These will be put in front of the doors of the bus and will be automatically opened so that no one can open it
  • This will not only protect people from falling in but also from dropping their belongings
Digital map with gps for trains
  • Just like the bus already has the train since it's already having more screens installed as of today in the future there could be a digital map installed having a gps signaling any stops
